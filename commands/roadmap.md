## How to Read a Roadmap

Each roadmap shows:

1. **Foundation** - Core knowledge everyone needs
2. **Core Skills** - Essential skills for the role
3. **Advanced Topics** - Deep dives into specializations
4. **Tools & Technologies** - Specific tools you'll use
5. **Learning Resources** - Where to learn (docs, courses, etc.)
6. **Timeline** - Expected learning duration
7. **Career Path** - How the role progresses

## How to Get Started

1. **Explore the roadmap** - See what's involved
2. **Identify your current level** - Where are you on the map?
3. **Find the next step** - What's the next logical skill?
4. **Set a goal** - Target a specific point on the roadmap
5. **Use the /learn command** - Get personalized guidance

## Tips for Using Roadmaps

- **Don't memorize** - Understand the map structure
- **Focus on foundations** - Master basics before advanced
- **Customize your path** - Not everyone needs everything
- **Check prerequisites** - Understand dependencies
- **Build projects** - Apply what you learn
- **Revisit periodically** - Roadmaps evolve as tech changes
